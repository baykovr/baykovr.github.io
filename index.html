Research

My research interest is in network and system security. I enjoy building practical and useful tools to address problems in this area. Much of my work is free and open source for anyone to use.

  • My current focus is in Software Defined Networking (SDN) security.
  • Mobile security (Android System).
  • Network scanning/surveying.
